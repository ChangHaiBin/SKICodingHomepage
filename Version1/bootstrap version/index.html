Math-Oriented Coding Lessons

S.K.I. Coding School offers a proprietary math-oriented coding program for students aged 10 to 18.

The lessons are designed for students who are well-versed in mathematics, e.g. participated in Math-Olympiad competition, or Secondary School and above.

Our math-focused curriculum allows us to focus on complex algorithm problems, and allows our talented students to achieve greater results in a shorter amount of time.

Dedicated Teacher

Our teacher, Mr. Chang, is equipped with technological knowledge and extensive mathematical background. He is dedicated to giving the best tools and training to talented students, to push their capabilities, and to help them connect their primary school math education to real life problems.

Our small class size and one-to-one lessons (available upon request) encourage student participation and interaction for better learning experience and result.
